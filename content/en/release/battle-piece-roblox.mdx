Stat allocation is another critical aspect of early game progression. <Section icon={Target} title="PvP Combat & Raid Preparation">

Once you approach the higher levels in Battle Piece, the focus shifts toward competitive PvP battles and coordinated raids. Success in these modes requires more than just high stats; you must understand positioning, cooldown management, and team composition.

PvP battles are highly dynamic. Because the game supports cross-platform play, you will face opponents on PC, mobile, and console. Each platform has distinct input methods, with PC players often utilizing hotkeys for faster transitions, while mobile and console players rely on optimized touch or controller layouts. Understanding these differences helps you predict opponent movements and counter their strategies.

In PvP, mastering the art of baiting is essential. This involves tricking your opponent into using their main defensive or escape options, leaving them vulnerable to your high-damage combos. Additionally, understanding the cooldowns of popular weapons and powers gives you a significant advantage. If you know an opponent has just used their primary escape move, you have a brief window to play aggressively.

Raids, on the other hand, require coordinated team play. Bosses in raids have massive health pools and devastating attacks that can easily defeat unprepared players. Bringing a balanced team with both high-damage dealers and crowd-control specialists increases your chances of success. Spatial awareness is key; bosses often perform large area-of-effect attacks that are telegraphed by visual indicators on the ground. Moving out of these danger zones immediately is more important than executing your damage rotation.

Before entering high-level raids or challenging experienced players, ensure your character is fully prepared. Use the checklist below to verify your readiness.
