<Section icon={Target} title="Raids & PvP Strategy">

Late-game progression in Battle Piece revolves around mastering raids and testing your skills in PvP matches. Both activities require a deep understanding of combat mechanics, but they demand entirely different tactical approaches.

| Preparation Step | Raid Requirement | PvP Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Level Target** | Close to level **600** cap | Level **400+** recommended |
| **Loadout Priority** | Wide-area damage and survival | High mobility and burst potential |
| **Network Setup** | Private server to avoid raid lag | Low-ping server for input accuracy |
| **Team Coordination** | High priority for group clearing | Optional, focus on individual spacing |

<Callout type="tip" title="Combat Spacing">
In both PvP and raids, greedy attacking will lead to rapid defeats. Learn to bait out opponent cooldowns before committing to your high-damage burst combos. Use your mobility skills defensively to reset the fight when necessary.
</Callout>

For raids, coordination with your team is vital. Ensure your group has a balance of crowd control to keep enemies grouped and high-damage dealers to burn down boss health bars. In PvP, focus entirely on individual spacing, learning the exact range of your opponent's weapons, and punishing their missed attacks.
