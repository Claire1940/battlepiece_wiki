### PvP Combat Tactics
Success in player-versus-player combat relies on spacing and reaction time. Because the game supports cross-platform play, PC players generally have an advantage in camera control, while mobile and console players must optimize their button layouts to ensure quick access to dodge maneuvers. Always monitor your opponent's cooldowns and strike immediately after they miss a high-damage ability.

### Raid Progression Strategies
Raids are designed for cooperative play and feature waves of powerful enemies followed by a final boss fight. Unlike standard bandit farming, raids require players to manage aggro and rotate defensive skills to survive high-damage boss phases. Team composition is critical; ensure your group has a mix of high-damage area-of-effect loadouts to clear waves quickly, alongside single-target burst builds to burn down the boss.
