<Section icon={Swords} title="Leveling Progression & Battle Piece Tips">

Progression in the Alpha stage is linear but requires efficiency to avoid unnecessary downtime. Your immediate goal is to scale your character toward the current Level Cap of 600. The most reliable method to achieve this is through systematic bandit farming. Bandits spawn in predictable groups, allowing players to gather them together and defeat them using area-of-effect abilities.

<Callout type="tip" title="Farming Efficiency">
Gather at least three to four bandits before executing your cooldowns. This maximizes your experience yield per ability rotation and reduces overall farming time.
</Callout>

To optimize your leveling path, follow this structured progression route:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initialize on a Stable Server">
    Launch the game from the [Official Roblox Experience](https://www.roblox.com/games/98662768290911/Battle-Piece). If public servers exhibit high latency, secure a private server to ensure your inputs register correctly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master the Basic Melee Combo">
    Practice your basic attacks on isolated bandits. Focus on learning the maximum range of your weapon and the recovery time after the final strike of your combo.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Group and Clear Bandits">
    Locate the primary bandit spawns. Walk near them to draw aggregation, pull them into a tight cluster, and deploy your highest-damage skills to clear them simultaneously.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Transition to Higher-Level Zones">
    Once your level exceeds the local bandit level by 50, locate the next farming zone. Do not stay in low-level areas, as the experience scaling drops off significantly.
  </Step>

<Section icon={Shield} title="PvP Combat Strategies">

Engaging other players in Battle Piece requires a transition from predictable PvE patterns to reactive combat. Spacing, prediction, and cooldown tracking are the pillars of successful PvP play. Since the game features cross-platform support, understanding your opponent's potential limitations based on their platform can give you a tactical edge.

<Callout type="warning" title="Combat Readiness">
Do not enter PvP arenas unprepared. Ensure you have reached at least Level 300 and have unlocked a mobility option to avoid being easily zoned by experienced players.
</Callout>

To optimize your combat setup, configure your platform settings according to the recommendations below:

| Platform | Strengths | Weaknesses | Optimization Tips |
|--- |--- |--- |--- |
| PC | Precise mouse aiming, rapid keybind access | Stationary setup | Use shift-lock for consistent camera tracking |
| Mobile | Highly portable, intuitive touch controls | Screen clutter, limited visibility | Customize UI scale to maximize screen space |
| Console | Comfortable controller ergonomics | Slower camera panning, button mapping | Adjust joystick sensitivity for faster turns |

When fighting, focus on baiting out your opponent's dodge or escape mechanics first. Once their defensive tools are on cooldown, execute your primary burst combo. Never commit your highest-damage abilities while the opponent has their evasion options ready.

</Section>
