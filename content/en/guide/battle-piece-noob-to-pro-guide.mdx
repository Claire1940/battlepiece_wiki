<Section icon={Target} title="Leveling Progression and Grinding Routes">

Reaching the level 600 cap requires a structured approach to farming. In the early stages, your main priority is accumulating experience points (EXP) by defeating bandits. Bandits provide a predictable pattern of attacks, making them the perfect practice targets for learning weapon ranges and cooldown timings.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initialize on a Private Server">
    If you experience high ping or lag, join a private server. The developers recommend private servers to ensure smooth input response during combat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master the Basic Bandit Farm">
    Locate the starter bandit camp. Pull two to three bandits at a time to maximize your area-of-effect (AoE) damage without getting overwhelmed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Weapon and Power Specs">
    As you level up, allocate points to unlock new skills. Do not hoard points; early-game skill unlocks drastically speed up your farming speed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Transition to Bosses and Raids">
    Once you reach level 400+, shift your focus from standard bandits to boss challenges and early raid entries to secure high-tier drops.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To streamline your journey, follow the recommended grinding path outlined below:

| Level Range | Target Enemies | Optimal Strategy | Key Unlock Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1 - 100** | Starter Bandits | Gather groups of 3, use basic melee combos | Weapon skill unlocks |
| **100 - 300** | Intermediate Bandits | Utilize AoE powers to clear waves quickly | Power specs leveling |
| **300 - 500** | Elite Bandits & Mini-Bosses | Focus on dodge timing and cooldown management | High-tier weapon drops |
| **500 - 600** | High-Level Raids | Form a team, coordinate crowd control skills | Level cap achievement |

<Callout type="warning" title="Warning">
Do not attempt high-level raids before reaching at least level 450. Entering raids under-leveled reduces your damage output significantly and makes you a liability to your team.
</Callout>
