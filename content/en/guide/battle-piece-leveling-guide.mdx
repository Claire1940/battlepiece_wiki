<Section icon={Swords} title="Weapons and Powers: Build Synergy for Fast Clearing">

Your leveling speed depends heavily on your character build. In Battle Piece, players combine weapons, powers, and racial traits to create unique combat profiles. While some builds excel at single-target duel scenarios, leveling requires setups that can clear multiple enemies simultaneously.
