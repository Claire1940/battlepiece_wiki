<Section icon={Target} title="First-Hour Progression Workflow">

The first hour of gameplay sets the foundation for your character's build. Beginners should prioritize rapid leveling through structured PvE combat rather than engaging in premature PvP battles. Focus on establishing a reliable farming routine and securing a basic weapon before attempting to unlock complex powers.

Follow these sequential steps to establish your character during the early stages of the game:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Platform & Settings">
    Launch the game on your preferred device. Adjust the graphics settings to ensure a stable frame rate, which is critical for executing precise dodges and landing skill combos during combat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire a Basic Melee Weapon">
    Locate the starter merchant or equipment vendor to secure your first basic weapon. This provides a reliable source of damage that does not rely on cooldowns or energy consumption.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate Bandit Farming">
    Locate the initial bandit camp near the starter spawn area. Focus on grouping small clusters of bandits together to maximize the efficiency of your area-of-effect attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Allocate Stat Points Wisely">
    As you level up, distribute your stat points to complement your preferred playstyle. Balancing defense and primary weapon damage is recommended for early-game survival.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Unlock Your First Power">
    Once you accumulate sufficient resources from bandit farming, invest in unlocking your first power or spec to expand your combat utility and mobility options.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To help track your progress, refer to the early-game progression table below to identify the most efficient farming targets based on your level range:

| Level Range | Recommended Target | Location Name | Primary Objective |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Levels 1–50** | Basic Bandits | Starter Island | Master basic attack combos and timing |
| **Levels 51–150** | Elite Bandits | Outpost Ruins | Unlock initial power skills and mobility |
| **Levels 151–300** | Desert Raiders | Arid Dunes | Optimize cooldown management |
| **Levels 301–600** | Raid Bosses | High-Tier Arenas | Reach Level Cap 600 and farm end-game gear |

<Callout type="tip" title="Farming Efficiency Tip">
When farming bandits, draw their attention and retreat slightly to gather them into a tight cluster. This allows you to damage multiple enemies simultaneously, cutting your leveling time in half.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics & Controls">

Combat in Battle Piece is fast-paced and relies heavily on spacing, timing, and skill synergy. Players must balance their use of physical weapons and supernatural powers to maintain pressure on opponents while managing their defensive options. Understanding the control scheme for your specific device is crucial for success.

  <Card title="Weapon Traits">

    - **Stun Potential**: Many weapon skills can interrupt enemy attacks and open windows for follow-up combos.
    - **Upgrade Paths**: Weapons can be upgraded or replaced as you progress through higher-level zones.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Power Traits">
    - **High Burst Damage**: Powers offer devastating magic or elemental skills that can clear groups of enemies.
    - **Mobility Options**: Many powers include dashes, teleports, or flight mechanics to escape danger.
    - **Cooldown Dependent**: Relying solely on powers can leave you vulnerable when your skills are on cooldown.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Racial & Passive Traits">
