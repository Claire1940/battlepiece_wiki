<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Leveling and Progression">

Progression in Battle Piece requires a balance of patient PvE grinding and active gear upgrading. To help you reach the Level Cap of 600 without wasting time on inefficient routes, follow this structured step-by-step pathway designed for new players.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Launch the Correct Roblox Experience">
    Ensure you join the official Battle Piece experience developed by Studio Multiverse. Avoid duplicate games or unverified mirrors that do not save your character data.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Grind Bandits for Initial Levels">
    Locate the starter island bandits. These enemies have predictable attack patterns and short respawn timers, making them ideal for practicing your basic attack chains.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Invest in Weapons and Powers">
    As you accumulate gold and experience, unlock your first power or weapon spec. Do not worry about finding a perfect meta build immediately; focus on utility.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Private Servers for Lag Reduction">
    If public servers become crowded or laggy, switch to a private server. This is highly recommended for farming bandits efficiently without competition.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Transition to PvP and Raids">
    Once you cross the mid-game threshold (around Level 300 to 400), begin participating in PvP battles and team raids to secure high-tier drops.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your leveling journey is divided into three distinct phases. Understanding what to prioritize during each phase will speed up your progression significantly.

| Progression Phase | Level Range | Main Target | Recommended Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Early Game** | 1 to 150 | Starter Bandits | Focus on learning melee range and weapon cooldowns |
| **Mid Game** | 151 to 450 | Elite Bandits & PvP | Upgrade your primary power and optimize stat points |
| **End Game** | 451 to 600 | Raids & Ranked PvP | Team up with other players to clear challenging raids |

<Callout type="tip" title="Stat Allocation Tip">
Avoid spreading your stat points too thinly. Focus on maximizing either your weapon damage or your power damage first to ensure you can defeat bandits quickly.
</Callout>
