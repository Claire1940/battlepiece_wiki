<Section icon={Shield} title="PvP Combat and Raid Strategy">

Competitive matches and raids require different tactical approaches. While PvP centers on baiting enemy dodges and executing precise combos, raids demand sustained damage output and team coordination.

For PvP, positioning is key. Use the environment to block ranged attacks and wait for your opponent to exhaust their mobility cooldowns before committing to an all-out attack. In contrast, raids require players to share aggro and cycle defensive abilities to keep the boss positioned in one place.
