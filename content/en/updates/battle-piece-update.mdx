<Section icon={Shield} title="PvP Combat and Raid Strategies">

PvP battles and challenging raids represent the endgame loop of Battle Piece. Success in these modes requires a deep understanding of combat mechanics, platform-specific controls, and connection stability.

### PvP Combat Guidelines
Combat against real players is fast-paced and punishes poorly timed skill activations. Keep the following strategies in mind:
- **Spacing is key**: Maintain a safe distance while your primary escape tools are on cooldown.
- **Patience over aggression**: Wait for your opponent to miss a high-commitment attack before initiating your burst combo.
- **Platform settings**: Ensure your camera sensitivity is adjusted to track fast-moving targets, especially if playing on mobile or console.

### Raid Preparation
Raids require sustained damage and coordinated movement. Unlike standard bandit farming, raid bosses possess high health pools and devastating area attacks.

| Raid Phase | Objective | Threat Level | Recommended Action |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Phase 1: Minions | Clear incoming waves | Low | Group enemies and use wide-area attacks |
| Phase 2: Mini-Boss | Defeat target quickly | Medium | Focus fire while dodging ground indicators |
| Phase 3: Raid Boss | Survive burst phases | High | Prioritize dodging over dealing damage |

<Callout type="warning" title="Lag Warning">
High latency can make dodging raid boss attacks nearly impossible. If your connection is unstable, consider hosting your raid runs on a private server to minimize input delay.
</Callout>
