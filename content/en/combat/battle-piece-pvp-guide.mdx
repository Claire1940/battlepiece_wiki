<Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Combos & Practical Matchup Tips">

Mastering PvP in Battle Piece requires an understanding of action priority and timing. Every attack has a recovery animation, and every dash has a brief cooldown. Winning fights consistently is about forcing your opponent to waste their defensive options so you can land your primary damage combo.

The table below breaks down the essential combat actions you should master, along with their tactical values and common counters.

| Combat Action | Primary Purpose | Tactical Value | How to Counter |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Basic Dash | Quick movement, dodging skill shots | Repositioning, escaping combos, closing distances | Predict the dash direction and use an area skill |
| Block Break | Defeating defensive players | Forces the opponent open, allows combo follow-ups | Dodge backward or interrupt with a fast light attack |
| Burst Skill | High immediate damage | Punishing missed attacks, finishing low-health targets | Activate a defensive shield or dash out of range |
| Disengage | Creating distance | Allows cooldowns to recover, resets the fight | Chase down using mobility-focused powers |
