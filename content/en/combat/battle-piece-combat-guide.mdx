<Section icon={Shield} title="PvP Combat Strategies & Matchup Tips">

PvP in Battle Piece is fast-paced and unforgiving. Unlike PvE enemies, real players will exploit your missed attacks and poorly timed dodges. Success in PvP requires a deep understanding of matchup dynamics, spacing, and defensive play.

Spacing is the foundation of PvP. You must learn the exact reach of your opponent's weapon and power set. Keep just out of their range while baiting them into launching an attack, then quickly dash in to punish their recovery frames.

| Archetype | Strength | Weakness | Counter Strategy |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Rushdown** | High pressure, fast combos | Vulnerable to counter-attacks | Keep distance, bait and punish |
| **Zoner** | Ranged attacks, area control | Weak in close-quarters combat | Close the gap with mobility tools |
| **Defensive** | High block strength, counter moves | Low offensive pressure | Use block breakers, play patiently |

<Callout type="warning" title="Latency Warning">
High ping can severely affect your ability to dodge and parry in PvP. If you notice delayed inputs, adjust your playstyle to be more predictive rather than reactive, or switch to a server with better connection quality.
</Callout>

Block management is another critical aspect of PvP. Holding your block indefinitely will lead to a guard break, leaving you stunned and open to massive damage. Mix up your defense by using dashes to reposition instead of relying solely on your block.

</Section>
