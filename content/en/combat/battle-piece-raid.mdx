<Section icon={Swords} title="Boss Mechanics and Tactics">

Every **battle piece raid** boss in the Alpha release features unique mechanics that require specific counters. While the Sukuna update introduced more complex "Domain" style threats, the general logic for boss fights remains consistent across the game's various challenges.

**Raid Combat Checklist:**
- **Monitor Stamina**: Never let your stamina bar hit zero; you need it for emergency dashes.
- **Telegraph Recognition**: Watch the boss's hands and eyes for glowing effects signaling an ultimate.
- **Positioning**: Stay behind the boss whenever possible to avoid frontal cone attacks.
- **Cooldown Management**: Do not spam all abilities at once; keep one mobility tool ready at all times.

| Boss Mechanic | Counter Action | Risk Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **AoE Ground Slam** | Jump or Dash away | High |
| **Targeted Projectile** | Side-step timing | Medium |
| **Ultimate Charge** | Break shield or Hide | Critical |
| **Minion Summons** | Swap to AoE weapons | Medium |

<Callout type="info" title="Alpha Updates">
As an Alpha game, boss hitboxes may occasionally be inconsistent. If a boss seems to hit you from across the map, it is likely a server-side lag issue rather than a planned mechanic.
</Callout>

</Section>
