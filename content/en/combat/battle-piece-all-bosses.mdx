<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Best Powers & Weapons for Boss Fights">

To consistently defeat late-game bosses, your build must be highly optimized. A successful build in Battle Piece harmonizes three elements: your active power spec, your equipped weapon, and your passive traits or race bonuses. Because late-game bosses feature rapid attack sequences and unblockable mechanics, mobility is universally valued over static defensive stats. The ability to quickly reposition allows you to survive devastating AoE attacks while keeping your primary damage skills on cooldown.

| Loadout Build | Tier | Boss Combat Role | Key Mechanic Synergy |
|---|---|---|---|
| Mobility + Burst | S | Primary DPS / Kiting | High speed escapes paired with rapid high-damage burst windows |
| Wide-Area PvE | A | Mob Clearing / Add Control | Large AoE sweeps to control minor spawns during raid phases |
| Defensive Spacing | B | Survival / Tanking | Solid block values, parry windows, and health regen traits |

When designing your boss-hunting build, consider the following tactical principles:
- **Cooldown Management**: Never exhaust all your mobility skills simultaneously. Always keep at least one dash or teleport ability off cooldown to escape sudden boss attacks.
- **Parrying vs. Dodging**: Standard physical attacks from bosses can usually be parried to stagger them. However, glowing elemental or magical attacks must be dodged entirely.
- **Latency Optimization**: As recommended on the official Roblox description page, high-ping players should use private servers to ensure inputs are registered accurately during tight parry windows.

<Callout type="tip" title="Combat Spacing">
Maintaining proper spacing is often more effective than active blocking. Position your character just outside the boss's physical reach to bait out attacks, then step forward to punish.
</Callout>
