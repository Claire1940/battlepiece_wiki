<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Raid Boss Strategy">

Conquering raid bosses in Battle Piece requires coordination, timing, and proper server setup. High latency can ruin combat inputs, making timing-based dodges nearly impossible. Follow this step-by-step strategy to maximize your chances of success during raid encounters.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Optimize Your Level and Stats">
    Do not attempt raids immediately upon unlocking them. Grind bandits and minor bosses until you are close to the level 600 cap to ensure your health pool can survive accidental hits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Secure a Private Server">
    High ping is the primary cause of failed raids. Use a private server to stabilize your connection, ensuring your dodge and attack inputs register without delay.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Clear Initial Minion Waves">
    Raid bosses are protected by waves of powerful minions. Use wide-area PvE skills to clear these enemies quickly before they surround and overwhelm your team.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Bait and Punish Boss Attacks">
    Keep your distance and wait for the boss to execute a heavy attack. Once the animation finishes, close the distance with a mobility tool, unleash your burst combo, and immediately retreat.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Choosing the correct server type makes a significant difference in combat execution. The comparison table below highlights the performance metrics between server options.
