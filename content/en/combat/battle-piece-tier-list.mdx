<Section icon={Shield} title="Mid-Tier and Niche Options (B & C Tier)">

B-Tier and C-Tier options are not necessarily useless, but they require much more effort to achieve the same results as their higher-ranked counterparts. B-Tier builds often excel in highly specific scenarios, such as 1v1 duels or defensive raid setups, but struggle during general progression. C-Tier builds suffer from mechanical limitations, such as slow animation startups or lack of movement options.

Defensive spacing builds, for example, allow players to survive longer in raids by keeping bosses at a distance. However, their lower damage output makes them incredibly slow for farming bandits, which delays your journey to the Level 600 cap. Similarly, single-target duel builds can lock down a single opponent in PvP but fail miserably when swarmed by multiple enemies in PvE modes.
