Different platforms offer distinct advantages. PC players typically benefit from precise mouse targeting, while console players enjoy comfortable movement tracking. Mobile players must focus on keeping their screen clear to maintain spatial awareness.

| Platform | Advantage | Disadvantage | Control Style |
|---|---|---|---|
| **PC** | Precise aiming, fast keybinds | Fixed posture | Keyboard & Mouse |
| **Mobile** | Portability, touch triggers | Screen clutter, high lag | Touch Controls |
| **Console** | Smooth movement analog | Slower cursor targeting | Gamepad |
