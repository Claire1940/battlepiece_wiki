<Section icon={Target} title="Starter Roadmap and Leveling Route">

Progression in Battle Piece requires a balance between efficient PvE farming and strategic stat allocation. Beginners should focus on reaching the level 600 cap before dedicating resources to high-end PvP loadouts. The following steps outline the recommended path for new players entering the game.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Launch and Server Selection">
    Open the game via the official Roblox platform. If you experience high ping or lag, consider joining a private server to ensure smooth inputs during early combat practice.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Bandit Farming Basics">
    Locate the starter island bandits. Defeating these enemies is the most efficient way to earn early experience points and familiarize yourself with weapon ranges and cooldowns.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Stat and Power Allocation">
    As you level up, invest your points into stats that complement your chosen weapon or power. Avoid spreading points too thinly across unrelated attributes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Transition to Raids">
    Once you approach mid-game levels, form teams to challenge raids. Raids provide high-tier rewards and serve as a bridge to competitive PvP combat.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Private Server Advantage">
Using a private server can significantly reduce latency. This is particularly helpful when practicing precise attack combos or farming grouped bandits.
</Callout>
