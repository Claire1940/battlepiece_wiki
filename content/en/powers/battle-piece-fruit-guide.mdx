<Section icon={Swords} title="Battle Piece Fruit Guide: Alpha Tier List">

This section of the battle piece fruit guide ranks the primary fruit and power archetypes based on their performance in the current Alpha build. Ratings are determined by how efficiently a loadout handles bandit farming, raid completion, and player-vs-player encounters.

S-Tier powers focus heavily on mobility and burst damage, which are the most valuable traits in high-level PvP and raid repositioning. A-Tier options provide excellent utility for leveling, while B and C-Tier options are generally too slow or specialized for general use.

### S-Tier: Mobility & Burst
These loadouts are the absolute gold standard in Battle Piece. They allow you to engage opponents instantly, deliver high-damage combos, and disengage before taking counter-damage. They are highly favored in PvP and are crucial for dodging boss mechanics in raids.

### A-Tier: Wide-Area PvE & Simple Melee
These powers are the backbone of the leveling process. With wide attack ranges, they allow you to group up multiple bandits and defeat them simultaneously. While they lack the extreme mobility of S-Tier builds, their farming speed is unmatched.

### B-Tier: Single-Target Duel & Defensive Spacing
These builds are highly effective in controlled, one-on-one scenarios but struggle when overwhelmed by multiple enemies. They are decent for boss fights where you can predict movements, but they slow down your general progression speed.

### C-Tier: Slow Low-Mobility Builds
These powers should be avoided unless you have no other options. Their long animation wind-ups and lack of movement tools make you an easy target for both bandits and enemy players.
