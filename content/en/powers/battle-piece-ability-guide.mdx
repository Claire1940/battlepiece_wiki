To build a reliable setup, you must follow a logical progression path. Rushing into high-level PvP with basic starter gear will only lead to frustration. Use the steps below to systematically assemble and test your build.

<Section icon={Target} title="PvP Combat & Raid Tactics">

Once you reach the higher levels, the focus of Battle Piece shifts toward competitive PvP battles and challenging raids. Both modes require a deep understanding of your ability cooldowns and enemy attack patterns, though they demand very different strategies.

| Game Mode | Key Objective | Recommended Build | Vital Stat |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| PvP Battles | Outmaneuver and defeat players | Mobility Burst + Cutlass | Speed / Agility |
| Challenging Raids | Clear waves and defeat bosses | Wide Area PvE + Greatsword | Defense / Stamina |

In PvP, spacing is everything. You must bait out your opponent's high-damage abilities before committing to an aggressive push. In contrast, raids require consistent, sustained damage and teamwork to clear waves of enemies before they overwhelm your party.

<Callout type="warning" title="PvP Spacing">
Never commit all your mobility options at the start of a fight. Keep at least one dash or escape ability off cooldown so you can retreat if your initial burst fails to connect.
</Callout>
