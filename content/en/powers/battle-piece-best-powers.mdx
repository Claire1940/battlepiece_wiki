<Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Strategies for PvP and Raids">

Combat in Battle Piece differs greatly between player-vs-player matches and cooperative raids. PvP requires quick reflexes, precise spacing, and the ability to bait out an opponent's dodge. Raids, on the other hand, demand sustained damage output, crowd control, and coordination with your team.

Understanding the rules of engagement for each mode will prevent unnecessary defeats.

| Combat Mode | Priority Objective | Key Power Attribute | Common Pitfall |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| PvP Battles | Outmaneuver the opponent | High mobility and stun | Wasting dodge cooldowns |
| Raids | Clear waves and defeat bosses | Large AoE and high DPS | Ignoring boss telegraphs |
| Bandit Farming | Maximize experience per hour | Low cooldown AoE | Pulling too many enemies |

When preparing for raids, ensure that your team has a mix of crowd control and high single-target damage. Bosses in late-game raids have large health pools that require coordinated burst windows to defeat.

<Callout type="warning" title="Lag Management">
High ping can ruin your dodge timing in PvP. If you notice players teleporting or your skills failing to register, disengage immediately and seek a server with a better connection.
</Callout>
