To build a truly formidable character, you must understand the distinction between the two primary spec categories: Power Specs and Weapon Specs. While Power Specs rely on energy and offer high-impact burst potential, Weapon Specs provide consistent, resource-free physical damage that serves as the backbone of your basic attack combos.

| Feature | Power Specs | Weapon Specs |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Source** | Energy-based magic and special moves | Physical swords, cutlasses, and melee styles |
| **Resource Cost** | Often requires energy and cooldown management | Low resource cost with consistent basic attacks |
| **Combat Range** | High versatility (Ranged, AOE, and Burst) | Primarily close-range to mid-range control |
| **Grinding Value** | Excellent for clearing grouped raid waves | Strong for single-target bandit farming |
| **Best Suited For** | High-pressure PvP and fast repositioning | Combo extensions and baseline defense |
