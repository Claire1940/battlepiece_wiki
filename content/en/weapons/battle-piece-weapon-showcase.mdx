Players generally categorize builds into three distinct archetypes: Melee-Focus, Mobility-Focus, and Defensive Spacing. Choosing the right archetype depends heavily on your preferred playstyle and whether you focus on PvP battles or cooperative raids.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Raid and PvP Weapon Strategies">

Endgame content in this experience is divided into two main categories: competitive PvP battles and cooperative raids. Each mode demands a different tactical approach to weapon selection. While a high-burst, single-target weapon might excel in a 1v1 duel, it can leave you vulnerable during intense raid waves where multiple enemies crowd the arena.

For PvP, focus on weapons that offer quick startup frames and reliable combo starters. You need to be able to punish your opponent's mistakes instantly. For raids, prioritize weapons with wide sweeping attacks and defensive utility to protect your teammates while clearing waves of enemies.
