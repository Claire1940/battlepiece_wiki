### S-Tier: Mobility & Burst Loadouts
Mobility and burst options reign supreme in the current meta. These loadouts allow you to close distances quickly in PvP and escape devastating area attacks in raids. Because positioning is critical in Battle Piece, weapons that offer dash attacks or rapid retreat options provide a significant tactical advantage.

### A-Tier: Wide-Area PvE & Simple Melee
A-Tier options are highly reliable for general progression. Wide-area PvE weapons excel at gathering and defeating groups of bandits, which is the fastest way to gain experience. Meanwhile, simple melee weapons offer straightforward, predictable hitboxes that are ideal for players learning the core combat loop.

### B-Tier: Single-Target Duel & Defensive Spacing
B-Tier loadouts are specialized tools. Single-target weapons deal high damage but require precise aim, making them difficult to use when surrounded by multiple enemies. Defensive spacing weapons focus on keeping opponents at bay, which is useful for survival but slows down your overall leveling speed.

### C-Tier: Slow & Low-Mobility Builds
Slow builds with limited mobility struggle in almost all game modes. Without speed or wide reach, these weapons leave you vulnerable to faster opponents in PvP and make it difficult to dodge boss mechanics during raids.
