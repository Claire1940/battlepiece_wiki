<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Spec Showcase and Combat Roles">

Specs represent the magical abilities and elemental powers that define your character build. Unlike standard weapons, specs rely heavily on energy management, cooldown rotation, and targeting accuracy. 

When planning your build, you should categorize specs by their primary combat roles. Some specs excel at keeping enemies at a distance, while others force you into close-quarters combat.

| Spec Category | Combat Style | Key Strength | Synergy Partner |
|---|---|---|---|
| Elemental Burst | Ranged Magic | High Area Damage | Heavy Melee Weapons |
| Mobility Focus | Dash & Chase | High Speed | Burst Cutlass Blades |
| Defensive Spacing | Zone Control | High Survival | Long-Reach Polearms |

Understanding these categories helps you avoid building a character with conflicting abilities. For example, combining a low-mobility weapon with a low-mobility spec can leave you vulnerable to fast-moving opponents in PvP arenas.

<Callout type="success" title="Synergy Bonus">
The most successful builds utilize specs to lock down targets or escape danger, leaving the primary weapon to deal consistent damage during cooldown windows.
</Callout>
